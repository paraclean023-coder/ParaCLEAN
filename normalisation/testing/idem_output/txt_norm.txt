Compilation of corpora sourced from Opus for the Spanish-Asturian language pair (es-ast):
Source datasets:
gnome_es-ast_20240423
kde4_es-ast_20240423
nllb_es-ast_20240423
tatoeba_es-ast_20240423
ubuntu_es-ast_20240423
wikimedia_es-ast_20240423


######################
Additional Information
######################

The data in this folder has been filtered by:
Labse alignment score >= 0.75
Lingua probability score for Spanish >= 0.5

No language identifier and hence no threshold has been applied for the Asturian language.
